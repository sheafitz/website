Shea Fitzpatrick is a Brooklyn-based writer and artist currently exploring forms that synthesize comics, poetry, design, and animation.

She is a recent graduate of Wesleyan University where she received a BA in American Studies with a self-designed concentration in Cultural Studies of Art.

Parametric Sketches

Series of illustrations made in Grasshopper by sourcing image data from Processing sketches. They were made with components in Grasshopper that read brightness data to determine the radii of spheres that are mapped onto a divided surface, and then colorized in Photoshop.

I Didn't Ask for the Habit (2018)

I Didn't Ask for the Habit is an abstract comic that seeks to create a looping, yet expansive, liminal space.

Experimental "Comics"

Ongoing research on innovations in comics through a collection of illustrations, poems, diagrams, graphic scores, interactive media, and designs that acknowledge, but reimagine, the traditional framework of comics.
